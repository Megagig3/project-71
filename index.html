Hello, this is the Qsi School of Atyrau, located in Atyrau, Kazakhstan. This School is a private School and an international School.




Campus life

Preschool Playground

Qsi Atyrau is a giant school with many facilities for the students. This mini playground is only for preschool during their recess.



School Library

The School Library has many books and many genres of books. You are allowed to have 2 or 3 books depending on your age and teacher.



Great Hall

The Great Hall is where everything happens, all the celebrations and festivals happen here.



School Achievements

Raising Money
The Jogathon was so much fun! There was fruits to eat and you could choose if you would like to walk, sprint, run, hop, or jog. You could talk to your friends while you are walking.

We earned 600,000 T which is about $1,000! We had so much FUN.


Aussie Rules
Aussie Rules is like american football but with a twist, there is a rope which the oppenent has to steal to get possesion of the ball instead of tackling someone.

This was so much fun and there were many turn around in the score. It is one of my favorite sports.
